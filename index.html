Events

Academic classes are started for 6th Semester 07 Feb 2023

To make the day more memorable we also welcome you all for the grand E-waste Collection and motivational speech program. Given that motivation is the root of all other abilities, qualities and tasks you possess and carry out, it is probably the most crucial component that one should build up. In addition to Motivational speech we are also collecting the E-waste(charger, earphones, Hard disk,wires & cables,.etc) from the students and faculties in collaboration with ERODE CAUVERY LIONS CLUB. This is the opportunity for us to dispose of our electronic waste showing our responsibility towards the environment. As Technology advances, we tend to accumulate a large number of outdated or broken electronic devices. The upcoming E-waste collection event is a convenient and responsible way to dispose of these items. The E-waste will be collected from Faculty’s in Faculty Quarters and also in the Chemistry lab. Those who are willing can give their E-waste in the respective venue or contact student coordinators for help. For hostel students it will be collected in their respective hostels in the evening time and for day scholar students it will be collected in the chemistry lab(AS-BLOCK) till 4 p.m. We understand that you may have a busy schedule, but we hope that you will be able to participate in this important event. FEB 11 2023 1:30PM (SATURDAY) at Vedhanayagam Auditorium

This addressment will manifest your new growth paths and emerging journeys. We would like to inform you at this juncture, that it is mandatory for all the eligible department students to attend the meeting without fail. Eligible Departments :- AIDS, CSBS, CSE, CT, ECE, EEE, EIE, ISE, IT
